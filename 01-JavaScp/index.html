Linkin Park

Linkin Park is an acclaimed American rock band formed in 1996, renowned for blending alternative rock, nu-metal, rap, and electronic music. They achieved global fame with their Diamond-certified debut, Hybrid Theory (2000), and have sold over 100 million records worldwide, led by the unique vocal contrast of Mike Shinoda and the late Chester Bennington
